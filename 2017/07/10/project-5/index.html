  • 5/2016 – Present

    Web Designer & Developer

Improved load time of all web pages

  • 10/2010 – 3/2012

    VisionCourse Media

    Web Designer

    Designed over 150 unique and user-friendly websites for a wide variety of clients. Performed the front-end development of multiple websites using XHTML, CSS, and jQuery. Utilized modern online marketing and design strategies to increase website traffic and conversion rates. Wrote content, crafted e-mail newsletters, designed logos, and established distinct brands for clients. Designed multiple web applications for high-profile clients, such as eBay, ProStores, and Magento. Provided e-commerce solutions that helped customers triple their monthly revenue.

  • 2/2010 - 9/2010

    National Fibromyalgia Association

    Graphic Designer

    Designed and managed production of a 72 page quarterly magazine titled, Fibromyalgia AWARE. Managed and re-designed content for two large websites, which contain thousands of pages. Developed and delivered 6 to 8 e-mail newsletters to a constituency of 80,000 each month. Created custom content for social media websites, including: Facebook, Twitter, YouTube. Produced and managed the content for a WordPress blog.

  • 9/2007 – 2/2010

    AIM Group

    Graphic Designer

    Oversaw the graphic design, marketing, printing, and web design of four successful Italian restaurants. Photographed food, designed menus, produced marketing collateral, and handled in-house printing. Produced 4 bi-monthly e-mail newsletters, edited press releases, and developed marketing campaigns. Re-designed and developed websites for all four restaurants and managed content on a weekly basis.
